Product

LedgerHaul provides a system for managing payroll logic, contractor settlements, and billing controls. It is designed to support growing businesses without adding operational complexity.

Payroll and settlements, handled cleanly

LedgerHaul supports multiple payroll models and settlement structures without forcing rigid workflows. Calculations run consistently in the background, producing clear gross-to-net results.

  • Per-mile, flat-rate, and percentage-based pay
  • Accessorials and adjustments
  • Deductions and offsets
  • Gross-to-net settlement output
  • Audit-safe line item tracking

Billing and subscriptions without friction

LedgerHaul manages billing and access control in the background, keeping features aligned with plan status. This reduces manual oversight and ensures predictable system behavior.

  • Subscription billing with per-employee scaling
  • Automated access control by plan
  • Payment status enforcement
  • Webhook-driven subscription state tracking

Works with your existing systems

LedgerHaul connects to payroll and billing tools so you can integrate without changing how your business runs.

  • Payroll integrations via secure connections
  • Billing and payments handled through trusted providers
  • Secure token handling
  • Designed to fit into existing operations

Clear visibility and control

LedgerHaul provides clear insight into payroll activity, settlements, and billing status. Information is organized so teams understand what’s happening without digging through systems or spreadsheets.

  • Clear gross-to-net summaries
  • Subscription and access visibility
  • Predictable system behavior
  • Reduced manual oversight

Built to support growth without added complexity

LedgerHaul brings payroll logic, settlements, and billing together in a structured system designed to scale as your business grows.
