LedgerHaul

Product

LedgerHaul provides a backend system for managing payroll logic, contractor settlements, and subscription enforcement. It is designed to integrate with existing operations and scale as complexity increases.

Payroll and settlements, handled cleanly

LedgerHaul supports multiple payroll models and settlement structures without forcing rigid workflows. Pay logic runs consistently in the background, producing clear gross-to-net results.

  • Per-mile, flat-rate, and percentage-based pay
  • Accessorials and adjustments
  • Deductions and offsets
  • Gross-to-net settlement output
  • Audit-safe line item tracking

Billing and subscriptions without friction

LedgerHaul manages subscription billing and access control in the background, ensuring features remain aligned with plan status. Billing logic is enforced automatically so teams don’t have to monitor it manually.

  • Subscription billing with per-employee scaling
  • Automated access control by plan
  • Payment status enforcement
  • Webhook-driven subscription state tracking

Integrations that fit your stack

LedgerHaul is designed to integrate with existing payroll and billing providers, allowing teams to connect core systems without reworking their operations.

  • Payroll integration via OAuth
  • Stripe-based billing and payments
  • Secure token handling
  • API-first architecture for extensibility

Clear visibility and control

LedgerHaul provides clear insight into payroll activity, settlements, and subscription status. Information is organized so teams can understand what’s happening without digging through systems or spreadsheets.

  • Clear gross-to-net summaries
  • Subscription and access visibility
  • Predictable system behavior
  • Reduced manual oversight

Built to support growth without added complexity

LedgerHaul brings payroll logic, settlements, and billing together in a structured backend system. It’s designed to support growing teams with clear rules, predictable behavior, and integrations that fit into existing operations.
